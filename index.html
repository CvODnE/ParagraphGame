How to Play

  1. Write or paste a paragraph in the text area and click Start Game.
  2. After a brief display, the paragraph will be hidden.
  3. You'll be shown the start of a random sentence from your paragraph.
  4. Type the next words that complete the sentence, as they appear in your paragraph.
  5. Press Check (or Enter) to submit your answer.
  6. Earn points and streaks for correct answers. Try to complete as many as you can!
